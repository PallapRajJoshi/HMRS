About Us

ApiNampa
Hotel

Welcome to Api Nampa! Situated in the picturesque Darchula district, our hotel offers a peaceful retreat near Gokuleshwor Airport and the tranquil Chamaliya River. Nestled between Baitadi and Darchula districts, Api Nampa invites you to experience the best of both worlds – comfort and natural beauty.

When it's time to find the ideal place to stay, whether it's a hotel, vacation rental, resort, apartment, guest house, or even a tree house, look no further – we've got you covered.
